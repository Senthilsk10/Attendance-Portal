Remote Attendance Marking

Efficiency:

Teachers can create session instances effortlessly on our user-friendly web platform. Students, in turn, can mark their attendance remotely, saving time and reducing the hassle of traditional methods.

Location-Based Verification:

Accuracy:

Worried about attendance fraud? Our system has you covered. Each session is linked to a specific location, ensuring that students can only mark their presence when physically present in the designated classroom. No more unauthorized attendance!

IP Address Blocking:

Security:

To add an extra layer of security, we employ IP address blocking. This prevents any misuse by ensuring that a single device cannot be used to mark attendance for multiple individuals, enhancing the integrity of the attendance data..
